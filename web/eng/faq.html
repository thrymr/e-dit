FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1) What does E-ditorials do?

E-ditorials brings together a group of international reporters and thinkers from Germany, Turkey, Palestine, France and the US. The E-ditorials team follows Turkish local newspapers and translates key Editorials into English, French, Arabic and German to increase access to the public debate in Turkey.

2) Why E-ditorials?

While numerous wires offer news from local newspapers around the world, E-ditorials offers access to a collective production of opinionated pieces by experts.

3) Who needs E-ditorials?

Following Turkey's growing influence in the Middle East and the aftermath of the Arab Spring, policy makers, think-tank organizations, international relations departments, political science professors and journalists have developed an increasing interest to predict Turkey's steps.

4) How frequently do you publish?

E-ditorials English is a daily publication of summaries, while E-ditorial German, E-ditorial Arabic and Editorial French are published weekly on Fridays.

5) How do I subscribe?

You can purchase a personal subscription or a group subscription by emailing subscription@e-ditorials.com
