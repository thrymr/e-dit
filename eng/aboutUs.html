ABOUT US

E-ditorials is a new generation approach to journalism in Turkey that abridges Turkey's opinion leaders with those of the world's. By producing weekly summaries of key Turkish Editorials and translating them into English, German, French and Arabic, E-ditorials aims to create a transnational public sphere.

Based in Istanbul, E-ditorials opens the door into who says what about social, political and economic issues in Turkey and the region, as well as shedding light on what to expect in the near future. With a team of international enthusiasts, E-ditorials contributes to the development of a new Middle East, and fastens the speed of info circulation.

The variety of E-ditorials encompasses all sides of the political spectrum in the name of objectivity and presents a wide and contrasting scope of ideas.
